About PourPurr

PourPurr is a fast-paced skill game where you pour milk into moving glasses. One wrong move and you're out.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

What We Don't Collect

Game Center

PourPurr uses Apple's Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple and follows Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your Game Center data.

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your game settings (cat color, game mode preference, sound settings)
  • Your high scores

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Common Questions

How do I play?
In Starter Mode, tap anywhere on the screen to pour milk. In Pro Mode, tilt your phone forward to pour. Fill each glass to the line‚Äînot too little, not too much!

How do I change game modes?
Tap the Settings button on the main menu, then select your preferred game mode from the dropdown.

How do I customize my cat?
Go to Settings and tap one of the colored squares to change your cat's color.

Why isn't Game Center working?
Make sure you're signed into Game Center in your device settings. Game Center features require an internet connection.
